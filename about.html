About

Ernsberger & Helmer, P.C. was founded by attorneys Marylyn Lewis-Ernsberger and Larry R. Helmer in October 2014. After both attorneys started business together at two previous law firms, the two decided to start the first law firm ever located on the square of Angola, Indiana. Bryan W. Lewis joined the firm in 2016.

Marylyn is a mother and former entreprenuer from Orland, Indiana. Marylyn is a 1985 graduate of Prairire Heights High School and a 1990 graduate of Indiana State University. She started Orland Video, a movie rental store, in Orland shortly after graduating from college and operated the business until she sold it in 2003 to start her legal education. She graduated from Thomas M. Cooley Law School in 2008. She has been married to her husband Dan Ernsberger, another Orland native, since 1993, and has two children.

Larry, a LaGrange, Indiana native, has been an attorney for almost 50 years. He is a 1967 graduate of Hanover College and later attended Indiana University Law School in Indianapolis. He was one of the first attorneys in Caramel, Indiana, and has also practiced in Florida. He has two children, four grandchildren, and has been with his partner Sandy Wilson for four years.

Bryan is a a graduate of Prairie Heights High School, Indiana State University, and Ohio Northern University. He is a former inscurance defense attorney in Las Vegas, Nevada. An Orland, Indiana native, he moved back to his hometown in August 2015 and began practicing in his sister, Marylyn's firm in early 2016. He has a wife, Kimberly Lewis, two sons, a step-daughter, and four grandchildren.
